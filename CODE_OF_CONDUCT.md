# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation respectful and inclusive.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful
- Be constructive
- Avoid harassment

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for enforcing standards.

## Scope
Applies to all contributors.

## Enforcement
Violations will be reviewed and addressed.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Warnings or bans may be applied.
